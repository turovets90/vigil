Stage 1
Buyer sends properly made on the company letterhead, stamped and signed LOI in the name of VIGIL.
download LOI in Word / download LOI in PDF
Stage 2
VIGIL LLC issues SCO.
Stage 3
Buyer issues ICPO
Stage 4
Seller issues FCO and draft of the Contract for confirming by the Buyer.
Stage 5
Buyer signs and sends the FCO and draft of the Contract with amendments, if any. At the same stage Buyer sends the draft of the financial instrument (SBLC, DLC).-
Stage 6
Seller check all the potential risks, confirms financial instrument and sends the original Contract to the Buyer.
Stage 7
Contract to be verified by the Seller and Buyer and signed the electronic version.
Stage 8
Buyer provides POF in form of BCL or RWA (bank to bank ONLY).
Stage 9
Seller conduct the SGS Inspection of the goods at the warehouse/storage and provides POP (bank to bank ONLY).
Stage 10
Buyer’s bank sends Previous financial instrument for acceptance to the Seller’s bank.
Stage 11
Seller’s bank confirms the acceptability of the terms and conditions of the financial instrument otherwise advise the amendments, if any.
Stage 12
Buyer’s bank issues Final financial instrument to the Seller’s bank
Stage 13
Seller’s bank issues P/B 2.0% in favor of the Buyer to the Buyer’s bank.0
Stage 14
Seller conduct the SGS Inspection of the goods at the port of loading and loads the cargo.
Stage 15
Seller sends original shipment documents to the Buyer’s bank and Buyer’s bank pays 100% invoice value via MT103 to the Seller’s bank.
Stage 16
Parties repeat all again in case when everyone is happy.
NOTE: The final Procedures will be indicated in the Seller’s FCO.
