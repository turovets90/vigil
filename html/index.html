ABOUT COMPANY
ANESTA LLC is an Ukrainian company specialized in the export of agricultural products from Ukraine.Our company represents of producers, traders and investors from Ukraine, which have guaranteed quality of the product and impeccable business reputation. We act as an authorized Representative and provide full professional support of the transactions between the Seller and Buyer. The Contract will be signed directly between the Seller and Buyer.
